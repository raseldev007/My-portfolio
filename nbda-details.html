NBDA

Blood Donation Management System

Project Overview

NBDA addresses the critical need for efficient blood donation management. It connects donors with those in need, streamlining the process of finding specific blood groups in specific locations. The app serves as a lifeline during emergencies, reducing the time taken to find a matching donor.

Key Features

  • Instant Blood Donor Search

  • Real-time Notifications for Requests

  • Donor Registration & Profile Management

  • Admin Dashboard for Verification

Technology Stack

Flutter Firebase Auth Firestore Google Maps
